About Me

Upon graduating from SUNY Oswego in June 2020 amidst the pandemic - and one drive-by graduation party later, I embarked on my career in tech at a specialized software company focused on Telecommunications analytics.

I began my career in sales and marketing focused on generating leads and client meetings. However, I was always determined to go beyond my job title and actively learned more about different aspects of technology and business. I took on managing customer accounts, providing input for new features, and creating fresh marketing strategies.

In 2021, I began to learn about Product Management. What interested me the most in the role was its demand for a versatile individual capable of wearing multiple hats within a company.

Today, I proudly serve as a Product Manager with a diverse set of responsibilities, overseeing not only marketing strategy but also the entire lifecycle of a SaaS platform, from its launch to growth and ongoing maintenance. Thankfully I am surrounded by lifelong learners and ambitious creators allowing me to fast-track my learning into product.

My experience has provided me with a firsthand perspective on the ever-evolving landscape of tech and am excited to witness the directions the industry will go in the near future.
