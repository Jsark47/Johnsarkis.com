About Me

After graduating in June 2020 with a degree in Information Science, I started my career at a specialized software company focused on telecom analytics.

Beginning my career in sales and marketing, I was focused on generating leads and onboarding new clients. However, I was always determined to go beyond my job title and actively learned more about different aspects of technology and business. I took on managing customer accounts, providing input for new features, and developing fresh marketing strategies.

In 2021, I took the next step into learning about Product Marketing. What interested me the most was the role's demand for a versatile individual capable of wearing multiple hats in a company from Web Design to Analytics and Marketing Strategy.

Today, I proudly serve as a Product Marketing Manager with a diverse set of responsibilities, overseeing not only marketing strategy but also the entire lifecycle of a SaaS platform, from its launch to growth and ongoing iterations. Thankfully I am surrounded by lifelong learners and ambitious creators allowing me to fast-track my learning into product.
